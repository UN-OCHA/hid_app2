Reset password

If you forgot the password to your Humanitarian ID account, or your password has expired, you can request a new password by entering your email address here and clicking "Reset Password". You will receive an email with a temporary link.

Note: Allow some time for the email to get to your inbox (especially if using @un.org domain). If you do not receive the email kindly do not reset the password a second time, but get in touch with us: info@humanitarian.id
