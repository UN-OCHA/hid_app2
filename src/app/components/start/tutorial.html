In the navigation bar you will find a search box. Type in it and you will see contacts and lists showing up.

If you got a lot of results, use the filter to fine-tune your search.

You can find the "Check-In" and "Check-Out" buttons on the right-hand side. Click for example on Check-in and you’ll be able to check into many lists by typing. You’ll be suggested with a number of other related lists you can also check-in.

If you only want to be temporarily part of a list, add a check-out date. This way we’ll remind you and remove you automatically of your check-out.

You can create your own contact lists from your dashboard. Once you’ve given your list a name and decided who can view and join it Display screen for settings, you can start adding and removing contacts from your list. Feel free to also export your contact list into PDF for example.
