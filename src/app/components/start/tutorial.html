In the navigation bar you will find a search box. Start typing your search item in the box and contacts and lists will immediately appear.

If you get a lot of results, use this filter to fine-tune your search.

You can find the Check-in and Check-out buttons on the top-right of your screen. To get started, click on Check-in, then start typing the name of the country, disaster, or group in the box. Select your list and then look at the options for other suitable lists.

If you want to join a list temporarily, add a check-out date. We’ll send you a reminder shortly before the given date and we’ll check you out automatically two weeks after the end-date.

You can create your own contact lists from the dashboard in the menu at the top of your screen. Once you’ve given your list a name and decided who can view and join it, you can start adding and removing contacts from your list.

Feel free to export your contact list into PDF or other available formats.
